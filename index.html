Sannara Ek - ឯក សាងណារ៉ា

I am currently a 3rd year PhD Candidate in the GETALP and M-PSI team at the Université Grenoble Alpes, supervised by Prof. Philippe Lalanda and Prof. François Portet. My thesis subject is on Federated Learning within a Pervasive Computing context where I develop lighweight ML solutions that are robust to hetereogenous environments.

I received my master degree specializing in computer vision and robotics from the Université Grenoble Alpes and Grenoble INP.

Activities

  • August 2024 — Participated in a research exchange at the Internet and Mobile Computing Laboratory (IMCL) - Hong Kong Polytechnic University to collaborate and extend my FL findings into the vision domain.
  • October 2022 — Participated in a research exchange at EveryWare Lab - University of Milan to collaborate on findings regarding pre-trained and self-supervised learning models in the wearable Human Activity Recognition domain.
  • November 2021 — Visited the Measurement and Electronic Architectures group - Institut Jean Lamour to investigate deep learning solutions for arc fault detection.

Supervision

  • June 2023 — Akram Chaouache's M1 thesis on Benchmarking self-supervised learning techniques for human activity recognition on mobile devices.
